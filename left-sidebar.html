History and Locations

REPEATZZA was founded in 1990 by John Schnatter as a family-run business. The business was founded with the purpose of being a backup plan in case his other pizza store did not work out. Luckily, he was right to start REPEATZZA because the other business was hurting themselves by raising prices on pizza. In 2010, REPEATZZA was sold for $10 billion dollars and has since been doing much better than all other pizza chains. Since 1990, REPEATZZA has been serving its customers fresh pizzas that have them coming back for more. Some say its a secret ingredient that keeps customers interested, and they are right - we just can't say what exactly the ingredient is for legal reasons. Come in and try our pizzas today!

REPEATZZA began in Seattle, Washington, with just 5 team members. The store gained popularity by being in downtown Seattle and needed to expand widely to accomodate their customers. Two more locations were founded in downtown Seattle and the business looked to other states. One store opened in Oregon in 2005 and two more opened in California in 2015. REPEATZZA then looked to expand internationally and opened a location in France. The business is currently looking into other countries so that customers can still enjoy the sensation of our food even when they go abroad!.
